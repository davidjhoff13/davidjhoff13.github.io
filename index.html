Bio

David is an independent communications consultant who provide strategic advice to CEOs and government agency leaders across media relations, thought leadership, digital strategy, and crisis communications.

He has experience as an advisor to a Cabinet secretary, a public relations agency leader, and a nationally known journalist.

Over the past decade, he has led strategic communications campaigns that increase awareness and engagement across a range of audiences.

His subject matter expertise spans politics, public affairs, and domestic policy. He has extensive experience working in the education sector from early childhood through adult education.

Among communications professionals, David has a unique skill set in collecting, analyzing, visualizing, and communicating about complex data.
